Smart Public Transportation Scheduling

Business Case Overview

Relevant SDG: Sustainable Cities and Communities (SDG 11)

Target: 11.2 - Ensure access to sustainable public transport systems

Indicator: 11.2.1 - Percentage of urban areas with access to public transport

Approach to Solve the Problem

The problem of smart public transportation scheduling can be solved using AI and real-time data. The solution uses optimization algorithms to improve the scheduling of buses and trains, reducing waiting times and ensuring that services run when they are most needed.

Key steps:

  • Collect real-time data on passenger flow and transport availability.
  • Use AI algorithms to optimize scheduling and improve route efficiency.
  • Implement dynamic scheduling updates based on traffic conditions and passenger demand.

Pros

  • Increased Efficiency: Reduces wait times and optimizes resources.
  • Improved Public Satisfaction: Provides more reliable transport services.
  • Cost-Effective: Optimized schedules can lead to reduced operational costs.

Cons

  • High Initial Setup Costs: Requires investment in AI and infrastructure.
  • Data Dependency: Relies on accurate and up-to-date data.
  • Adaptation Period: May take time for public to adjust to new schedules.
Waste and Water Management System

Business Case Overview

Relevant SDG: Clean Water and Sanitation (SDG 6)

Target: 6.3 - Improve water quality and reduce pollution

Indicator: 6.3.1 - Proportion of wastewater treated safely

Approach to Solve the Problem

The solution focuses on optimizing the waste and water management systems using IoT sensors and real-time data analytics. By monitoring water quality, optimizing waste collection routes, and ensuring the safe treatment of wastewater, the system can greatly improve both water and waste management efficiency.

Key steps:

  • Monitor water quality with sensors and real-time data.
  • Use optimization algorithms to enhance waste collection routes and schedules.
  • Ensure safe and efficient treatment of wastewater using automated systems.
